Featured Poets

William Wordsworth

One of the most famous poets of the Romantic era, known for his work "The Prelude."

Emily Dickinson

Renowned for her unique style and reclusive life, Emily Dickinson's work has had a lasting impact on poetry.

Robert Frost

Famous for his depictions of rural life and his use of American colloquial speech in his poetry.

Maya Angelou

A civil rights activist and poet, Maya Angelou's works have inspired millions.

Langston Hughes

A leading figure of the Harlem Renaissance, known for his insightful portrayals of African-American life.

Sylvia Plath

Known for her confessional style of poetry, Sylvia Plath's work explores themes of mental illness and identity.
